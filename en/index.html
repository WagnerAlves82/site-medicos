Our Mission

Empowering Brazilian Christians to make a real difference: funding doctors who work on the front lines in Israel, providing humanitarian relief, unmasking media lies, and fulfilling the biblical calling to love our neighbor.

Why should I contribute

The children of darkness are more agile than those of light, because evil often acts with unity and speed, while good hesitates. Today, we see nations turning against Israel, defending what is unjust and forgetting the cruel attack that took the lives of more than a thousand innocents. Israel is the land of the patriarchs, the cradle of faith and the home of Jesus. Yet, the chosen people suffer. It is time to act with faith, love and courage.

Stories that Transform

Three victories that show how God fights for Israel and leads His people to victory, even against all odds.

The Angel Who Defeated an Army

When King Sennacherib besieged Jerusalem, God sent an angel who destroyed 185,000 Assyrian soldiers in a single night. (2 Kings 19:35)

Victory belongs to the Lord.

David and Goliath

A young shepherd faced the Philistine giant with only a sling and faith in the God of Israel. (1 Samuel 17)

With God, the impossible becomes victory.

Gideon and the 300

With only 300 men, Gideon defeated thousands of Midianites — because God was leading the battle. (Judges 7)

God doesn't need many, just faith.
